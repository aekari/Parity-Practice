Parity is a fundamental concept in mathematics. The parity of an integer is its attribute of being even or odd.

Addition

  • Even + Even = Even
  • Even + Odd = Odd
  • Odd + Odd = Even

Subtraction

  • Even - Even = Even
  • Even - Odd = Odd
  • Odd - Even = Odd
  • Odd - Odd = Even

Multiplication

  • Even x Even = Even
  • Even x Odd = Even
  • Odd x Odd = Odd

Division

When you divide whole numbers, you might not always get another whole number. For instance, 1 divided by 4 equals 1/4, which isn't even or odd because those terms only apply to whole numbers.
